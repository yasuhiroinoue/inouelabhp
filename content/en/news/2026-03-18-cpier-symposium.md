Prof. Inoue will participate as an invited speaker in a unique "role-playing interview" session at the *Kyoto University 100-Person Paper Symposium 2025 After-Party*!

Hosted by the Center for the Promotion of Interdisciplinary Education and Research (CPIER), this event will take place on March 19, 2026, and features speakers exchanging roles for an entertaining and insightful academic discussion.

For more details about the event, please visit the [official CPIER website](https://www.cpier.kyoto-u.ac.jp/update/100nin2025_after_sympo/).
