A collaborative research article with Dr. Adachi (Keio Univ.), Prof. Kondo (NIG), and our lab member Dr. Morikawa has been published in Zoological Letters.

This study investigates the mechanisms underlying carapace morphogenesis during the metamorphosis of the Japanese spiny lobster from phyllosoma to puerulus.

[https://doi.org/10.1186/s40851-026-00265-8](https://doi.org/10.1186/s40851-026-00265-8)
