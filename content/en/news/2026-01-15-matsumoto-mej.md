Collaborative research results by Yoshihiko Matsumoto and Prof. Yamasaki (Waseda University) have been published in the Mechanical Engineering Journal.
We proposed a design method for one-degree-of-freedom planar link leg mechanisms for multi-objectives through NSGA-II.

Yoshihiko MATSUMOTO, Shintaro YAMASAKI, Yasuhiro INOUE,
One-degree-of-freedom planer link leg mechanisms design method for multi objectives through NSGA-II,
Mechanical Engineering Journal,
[https://doi.org/10.1299/mej.25-00278](https://doi.org/10.1299/mej.25-00278)
